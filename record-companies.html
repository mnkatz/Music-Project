Record Companies

In the development of streaming services, record labels want the same thing as artists - fair compensation from the companies that host the music. Record labels have been able to set specific royalties in their deals with artists in the past, taking a certain cut of each album sale. However, with variability of compensation across each streaming service, record labels have had to stay nimble. In light of these changes, they must set new deals with artists in which they are able to profit from distributing the music and in which they incentivize the artist to make more music.
